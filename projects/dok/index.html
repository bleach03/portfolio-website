Dread of Knight
November 2023   —   December 2023
Trapped in a decrepit castle surrounded by vast woods, a knight searches for an escape—only to find every exit locked. As knights pass in the seemingly empty halls, strange dreams begin to haunt him: visions of life as a distant king. What he doesn't know is that, far away, the king dreams of being the knight. This game was made in 2 weeks using the Unity engine, C# for scripting, Aseprite for modeling, and FL Studio for sounds. Click on the title to check out the game design document.
