You're Out!
January 2025
After several complaints from tenants and upper management, you get a phone
call from the board; You're out. Naturally, you get mad. That's when you decide
to wreak havoc and kick out every last tenant in the building.
Of course, no one likes that, and people are calling the cops on you, so be fast
and finish the work day! There's a single mother of two out there to evict!
This game was made in 1 week for the Beginner's Jam Winter 2024, placing 3rd.
Play as an evil Landlord in this funny/parody typing game, and evict as many
tenants from your apartment as possible!
Process: My team and I coded this in Godot. I led the creative direction and
created models/textures as well. I wanted to create a game that had a speedrunning
component, because I really like speedrunning, so my team and I settled on a
TypeRacer inspired game. However, I did wanted to mix the functionality of a 3D
game, so we decided to add a maze component. We are still working on the game, and
are working on adding a horror aspect. Essentially, we set out to create an
interesting almagamation of what we love in games.
